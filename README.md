## What is this?
This is M5StickC metronome with viberaion and light and click sound.

## Usage
### BtnA('M5')
Start/Stop metronome.  

### PowerBtn(upper side)
Tempo Up or Volume Up.  

### BtnB(lower side)
Tempo down or Volume Down.  
Long press to change tempo cursol.
